Section 1 presents the foundations of the project and places the rest of the documentation in context. It first describes the IMPaCT cohort and its longitudinal wearable-based monitoring approach; then explains the MATRIX device used for signal acquisition; and finally summarizes the WearablePerMed project and its goal of transferring these developments into a clinically useful setting for primary care.
